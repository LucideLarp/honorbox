**30 days, no questions asked — on everything sold here.**

If anything you bought from this store — [HonorBox Pro](./honorbox-pro.html)
or [Crew](./crew.html) — isn't what you wanted, for any reason or none, you
get your money back within 30 days of purchase.

## How

Any of these works:

1. Email **honorbox@proton.me** with the address you used at checkout.
2. Reply to your Stripe receipt email and say "refund".
3. Open an issue on the [public repository](https://github.com/Honorboxx/honorbox/issues)
   titled "Refund" — you don't need to explain anything.

Refunds are issued through Stripe to your original payment method, usually
within one business day of the request being seen.

## What happens on refund

Repository access is revoked and the license ends — that's the fair trade
for a no-questions policy. Refunds are honored in good faith; accounts that
buy, clone, and refund repeatedly will be declined and handled through Stripe.

## After 30 days

Ask anyway. If something is genuinely broken and can't be fixed, being past the
window won't be used against you.
