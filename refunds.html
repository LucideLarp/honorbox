Refunds

30 days, no questions asked.

If HonorBox Pro isn't what you wanted — for any reason, or none — you get your money back within 30 days of purchase.

How

Any of these works:

  1. Email honorbox@proton.me with the address you used at checkout.
  2. Reply to your Stripe receipt email and say "refund".
  3. Open an issue on the public repository

titled "Refund" — you don't need to explain anything.

Refunds are issued through Stripe to your original payment method, usually within one business day of the request being seen.

What happens on refund

Repository access is revoked and the Pro license ends — that's the fair trade for a no-questions policy. Refunds are honored in good faith; accounts that buy, clone, and refund repeatedly will be declined and handled through Stripe.

After 30 days

Ask anyway. If something is genuinely broken and can't be fixed, being past the window won't be used against you.
